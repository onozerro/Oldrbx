Privacy Information

Last Updated September 12, 2025

Here's a list of the type of data we store, as well as how you can delete it.

  • If all you do is visit the website (without performing any actions like logging in or submitting an application), the only data stored is your user agent (sent automatically by your browser - it usually contains your browser, its version, and your operating system). This data is cleared roughly every 3 months and is used purely for detecting bots and other malicious users.
  • If you submit an application, all the questions and your answers are saved, even if your application is rejected. Your hashed IP address (hashed using SHA512 and various salts) is also stored temporarily to detect spam. Your IP is automatically deleted about 5 minutes after you submit an application.
  • If your application is accepted and you create an account, your username and password are both stored (passwords hashed using argon2), along with account creation data and any on-site data generated at creation time (such as your avatar and its thumbnail). Your hashed IP address is stored for about 1 hour, and we keep a count of created accounts (the IP is never directly linked to accounts).
  • If you log in to an account, your hashed IP address is stored for about 1 hour, and we track login attempts (the IP is not directly linked to accounts).
  • If you join a game, your IP address is embedded in a JWT sent to your browser. The client/server authentication system uses this to verify the browser that created the ticket is the same client playing the game. Although this IP is not stored on our servers, sharing the ticket could reveal your IP to others.

Any information you directly input into the website (such as descriptions or status updates) is saved. Every status update you make is stored permanently; only your most recent description is kept. Outfits you create, your latest avatar and its thumbnail, and any uploaded content (images, etc.) are saved to the site. Uploaded content may remain unless removed for violating our terms of service or is explicitly deleted.

You can request an account deletion by clicking here. You must be offline for one week before deletion; your last online is updated when you visit while logged in, so log out and schedule deletion after a week.

OLDRBX makes use of HCaptcha, Cloudflare, and jsDelivr, which have their own privacy policies.
