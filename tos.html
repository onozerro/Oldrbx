Terms of Service

Last Updated October 19, 2025

This is essentially just a list of rules for our platform. We reserve the right to update these terms at any time without notice - check here periodically. For privacy information, click here.

A violation of any of these rules may result in a temporary or permanent ban. New accounts will often receive permanent bans on first infractions, while established accounts may receive temporary bans depending on severity.

Text Guidelines

These apply everywhere on-site, such as in-game chat, group walls and etc.

  • Do not discriminate or harass people. Light swearing is permitted, but slurs used as insults are not allowed.
  • Do not reveal personal information - your own or others'.
  • Avoid starting drama. Do not create posts "exposing" someone. If you are concerned about a user, report it to a moderator.
  • Do not advertise off-platform content. Anything mentioning or related to OLDRBX is fine; spamming "join my site" or promoting unrelated channels is not and will lead to bans.

Image Guidelines

In addition to the text rules above, these rules apply to images uploaded to the site:

  • No violent or gory content.
  • Do not upload images containing real people (exceptions may be made for public figures where identity is obvious).
  • No pornographic content.

Other Notes

  • You are responsible for your account security. Password resets and account recovery are handled case-by-case - choose a strong password and keep it safe.
  • Advertising rules: Do not use our ad system to promote irrelevant external content (unknown sites, personal channels, etc.). Ads should be relevant to OLDRBX and any content on-site.

If you believe a ban is in error, please make a ticket in our Discord.
